Meet Our Team

The like-minded individuals who keep P2P moving forward.

Leadership Team

The two visionaries that started it all

Noodle

Founder | Rail Operations Head

Noodle, also known as Noodle_Developer on Roblox, founded P2P in March, where he began work on Gen1. Later that year in August, Noodle collaborated with Thyssen to create Gen2. After they had finished, P2P was revamped to P2P Studios, and Noodle and Thyssen remain as the two Founders of P2P Studios, continuing to lead and develop to this day. Noodle is one of two main scripters for P2P Studios, and is leading the development of Project Odyssey, and videos.

Lincoln

Founder | Bus Operations Head

Lincoln, known online as “thyssenkrupp234”, discovered his passion for programming at an early age. Over time, his curiosity evolved into a pursuit of creating hyper-realistic digital simulations that capture the intricacies of the real world. Fueled by his lifelong fascination with transit, Lincoln combined technical expertise with personal passion, leading to a collaboration with Noodle (“Noodle_Developer”). Together, they developed what is now recognized as the most realistic transit simulator in North America.

Executive Team

The executives and department leaders who keep us moving forward

Dextro

Chief Operating Officer

Dextro, formerly known as ItsDestructoProz, began in Ro-State - responsible for the startup of various law enforcement groups before discovering Ro-Transit due to his passion for public transportation. With over 5+ years of experience, he has developed expertise in both management and law. His diverse background allowed him to experiment with different asset development methods building his passion for game development and design while balancing the responsibility of being a team leader.

Nolan

Head of Operations

Nolan, known in the community as Rozzum0516, now serves as Head of Operations at P2P Studios. First introduced to the Ro-Transit scene by Linc in 2022, he briefly stepped away in 2023 before returning in early 2025, rejoining as an Executive. Since then, Nolan has played a pivotal role in shaping the studio’s direction, managing operations with a balance of creativity and strategy. He continues to contribute his passion for photography, video editing, and all things Apple to the team.

Aerich (Irish)

Map Development

Irish is a rising developer in the community, quickly establishing himself as the lead for map development at Ro-Translink. His expertise in building design, urban layout, and scenic detailing ensures that every location feels authentic and true to life. From major hubs like UBC Exchange to key SkyTrain stations such as Nanaimo and Joyce-Collingwood, Irish has delivered highly detailed recreations that enhance immersion. His precision and dedication bring Vancouver’s transit system to life in remarkable detail.
