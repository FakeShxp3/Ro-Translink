About Us

Our Mission

P2P Studios was founded by Thyssen and Noodle with two goals in mind: Do what others can't, and have fun.

So far, we've been highly successful - innovating further than what people had thought was "impossible", and in record timeframes. We strive to make our experiences and products as realistic, stable, and immersive as possible.

People

P2P Studios was founded by two teenagers, who are still leading the company forwards as acting owners. Noodle leads rail operations, and Thyssen leads bus operations - both of them oversee development and script P2P's highly-regarded line of vehicle chassis systems. We have a strong executive team to assist or lead departments, as well as a developer & supervisor team. Visit our Team Members page to learn more.

Projects & Games

P2P Studios has a unique array of products and games. We have a recreation of TransLink's INIT ("TMAC") system, a revolutionary bus chassis, an incredibly complex route management system, lag-free & advanced AI Passengers, and more. P2P has game built from scratch, directly under P2P Studios - Ro-Translink, a recreation of Vancouver's transit system. Other games, such as BRUG's MBTA, Suburban's Halifax, and our BRT acquisition will utilize P2P Products. Visit our Projects Page to learn more.
