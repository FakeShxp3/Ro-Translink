Team Members

Noodle (@Noodle_Developer)

Founder & Rail Operations Head

Noodle, also known as Noodle_Developer on Roblox, founded P2P in March, where he began work on Gen1. Later that year in August, Noodle collaborated with Thyssen to create Gen2. After they had finished, P2P was revamped to P2P Studios, and Noodle and Thyssen remain as the two Founders of P2P Studios, continuing to lead and develop to this day. Noodle is one of two main scripters for P2P Studios.

Thyssen (@thyssenkrupp234)

Founder & Bus Operations Head

Thyssen started out in the transit community when he was 12, and quickly gained a bad reputation for stealing TranslinkGuy's destination setter GUI. Determined to learn from his mistakes, he began working on NextWave's revamped chassis. After a successful launch, he began work on a custom chassis named 'OCv3', however he later dropped the project after he began work on P2P's Gen2 with Noodle in August of 2024.

Suburban (@Prebrizzle1902)

Executive Team

Suburban, also known as Kushintinmonte1997, is one of the most talented bus modellers in the North American Ro-Transit space. Suburban started off as a developer at Halifax Transit, climbing the ranks over time, and eventually replacing the now retired RobDevz. Nowadays, Suburban is making extremely realistic bus models that P2P Studios and Halifax will/are using, and developing Halifax's new divsion.

Tzu (@tzu_transit)

Executive Team

Tzu, a talented road & bus developer, has been a huge help to the Ro-Translink project and P2P Studios in general. Tzu started off as a developer for GVTA, a TransLink group founded in 2022, and shortly after, worked at MVT, another TransLink group founded in 2022. Now, Tzu is SCBCTA's Lead Developer, and an important member on our executive team, commended for his contributions to Ro-Translink.
