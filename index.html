Welcome to Ro-Translink

Ro-Translink is a meticulous 1:1 recreation of Metro Vancouver's transit system, offering an unparalleled simulation experience on Roblox. Drive buses through accurately modeled streets, operate SkyTrain lines, and interact with realistic passengers in a dynamic, evolving world.

Our game features cutting-edge technology including AI passengers, realistic vehicle physics, and true-to-life scheduling systems that bring the authentic transit experience to life.
